Mapo Tofu

Description

A classic Sichuan dish that is delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1 lb tofu (any firmness)
  • 3 oz chicken

During Cooking

  • 1 tbsp bean sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 chili pepper (chili sauce also works as a substitute)
  • 2 garlic clove
  • 1 green onion (garnish)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Chicken Marinade

  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp water

Texture & Flavor

  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp water
